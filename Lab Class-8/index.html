RajshahiNew

Rajshahi, a city in western Bangladesh, is the divisional headquarters of Rajshahi Division and an administrative district. Known for its silk industries and fruits, particularly mangoes and litchis, it's often referred to as "Silk City" and "Education City" due to its quality silk and numerous educational institutions. Located on the northern banks of the Padma River, Rajshahi is a major urban, administrative, commercial, and educational center in Bangladesh

Comilla: Rich in heritage, Comilla is home to ancient sites like Mainamati’s Buddhist ruins. The calm of Comilla’s countryside blends with its growing urban charm. Its history, cuisine, and academic legacy make it a cultural gem of Bangladesh.
